As you know, many predictors of happiness have been studied, but we know
surprisingly little about the effect of energy use on happiness. Energy is one
of the most fundamenal types of consumption and has an enormous effect on our
lives, which we take for granted in the developed world.  At the same time, 
 most of energy still uses up nonrenewable fossil fuels and pollutes. A key
 barrier to energy conservation is widely held belief that reduction in energy
 consumption will adversly affect wellbeing. We add one more argument for energy conservation--energy does not contribute much to happiness.     

Sincerely,\\
